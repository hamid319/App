#
APP CONCEPT

A location-based travel app where users receive recommendations (places & activities) based on their location and preferences.
Users swipe through places Tinder-style (right = like, left = skip).
They can save favorites, create shared groups with friends, and see which places the group mutually likes.
Users can tap a place → click “Route” → and open Apple Maps or Google Maps via deep linking.

This project's code must be simple, scalable, and easy to extend.
